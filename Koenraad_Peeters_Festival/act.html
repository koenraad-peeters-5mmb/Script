SAT 1

JOOST KLEIN

Worldwide-known artist from Fryslân, The Netherlands. Joost Klein started making Call of Duty montages at age 7. After this he fell in love with the internet. Joost combines this love with his adoration for 'weird' music. One of his biggest inspirations is Crazy Frog. Joost makes all of his visuals himself. Don’t take his laptop away from him! Scandinavian Boy (2017) was his first mixtape. He purely made this collection of music to perform. Since a kid he wanted to be on a stage. And these days he is known for bringing the most energy you’ve ever seen! Pukkelpop, Rock Werchter, Lowlands, Pinkpop & many more big festivals know about the crazy experience he delivers. He did the support act for Yung Gravy in Zurich, Switzerland and rocked the stage with Bbno$ in Germany. Russian Village Boys took him on tour in 2022. His rise to fame is just getting started in Europe, but you’re just in time to join the ride! After dropping his first album Albino (2019) in collaboration with Tantu Beats his stocks went up. Now these two dudes are inseparable. 1983 (2019) and Fryslân (2022) The world is a crazy place, but it’s even crazier when Joost Klein & Tantu come together. Joost started his own record label ‘Albino Sports’ in 2019.
